# Spec Product Lock Checklist

Lock these before marking the product domain complete:

- Product definition and primary value proposition
- Primary and secondary audience
- Trust inputs, claims, disclaimers, and sensitive-domain constraints
- Primary conversion and post-conversion expectation
- In-scope and out-of-scope boundaries
- Launch success criteria and phase-2 cuts
- Route groups and content ownership
- Discoverability and indexing posture

On update passes:

- preserve unchanged product truth explicitly
- record what changed and why
- call out any required downstream UX, UI, or tech reruns
