# Spec Product Intake

Use this intake to lock product truth. Trim it to the product in front of you.

## Identity and audience

- What are we building?
- Who is it for?
- Why would this audience trust it?
- What proof, claims, or credentials matter?

## Offer and scope

- What is the primary thing the user should do?
- What is in scope for launch?
- What is explicitly out of scope?
- What objections must be answered before conversion?

## Route and content shape

- What public, authenticated, admin, or operator route groups must exist?
- What content types or page types matter?
- Who owns content updates after launch?
- Which assets or copy are launch dependencies?

## Discoverability and trust

- Is acquisition search-dependent, referral-driven, or private?
- What indexing or noindex boundaries matter?
- Are there legal, privacy, moderation, or sensitive-domain constraints?

## Launch discipline

- What would count as launch success?
- What must be true before the product can ship?
- Which unresolved items are acceptable assumptions versus blockers?
