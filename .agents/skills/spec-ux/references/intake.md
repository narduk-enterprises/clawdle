# Spec UX Intake

Use this intake to lock flows and route-state behavior.

## Core flows

- What is the first-visit path?
- What is the primary conversion, onboarding, or activation flow?
- What does returning usage look like?
- What happens after login, signup, confirmation, or callback?

## Route-state behavior

- Which loading, empty, error, success, disabled, or retry states matter?
- Which states are route-level versus component-level?
- What support, recovery, or escalation path is required?

## Access and control

- Which routes are public, authenticated, admin, or operator-only?
- What redirect behavior is expected for protected routes?
- Does the user ever move between an auth authority and the app?

## Accessibility and feedback

- What accessibility level or commitments matter beyond baseline?
- Are reduced motion or dynamic text-size constraints important?
- What feedback must feel immediate, reassuring, or explicit?
